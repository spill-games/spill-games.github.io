Fruit Merge

The ultimate fusion of strategy and relaxation! Dive into a world of juicy fruits and addictive puzzles where your goal is to merge similar fruits to create new ones and climb the ladder to fruitful victory.
Drop Puzzle, your task is simple yet challenging: drop fruits strategically onto the board and watch them merge when they collide with others of the same kind. As you successfully merge fruits, you'll unlock higher tiers of fruits, each more delicious and rewarding than the last. But beware, as the board fills up, you must stay well below the spillover limit to keep the game going!

Shikaku Master

Shikaku Master is a captivating puzzle experience where your objective is to fill the grid with rectangles, following numerical clues without any overlapping shapes. With varying grid sizes and difficulty levels, each puzzle demands strategic thinking and precise placement to solve.
Shikaku Master offers a cerebral and enjoyable puzzle-solving journey. Download now and immerse yourself in the world of Shikaku puzzles!
