Bird Rescue Jam: Sort Puzzle

In this rescue block puzzle game, you need to complete brain teasing parking challenges, shoot bubbles down, solve the bird puzzle, and defeat the evil crocodile.This exciting rescue adventure is perfect for players of all ages who enjoy quick reflex games, puzzle challenges, and survival adventures. Every level is a colorful puzzle jam filled with danger, traps, and clever mechanics. Slide the ammo boxes to use your cannon to blast the bubbles and defeat the evil crocodile. Help the birds reach the treehouse and clear the level.

HOW TO PLAY:

Tap and slide​​ the ammo ​​box that ​​matches the bubbles color to fire the cannon and free the birds Each cannon shot destroys a bubble and delays the birds from getting caught by the hungry crocodile Help all the birds reach the treehouse and clear the level.

KEY FEATURES:

Fun and easy to play controls Exciting rescue missions with increasing difficulty Colorful graphics and engaging sound effects Endless challenges for all ages Download now to begin your epic journey through bird puzzles and rescue challenges!. With its perfect blend of parking puzzles and color matching. This game ensures fun for everyone from kids to adults.
