California Privacy Addendum

← Back to Privacy Policy

This Addendum applies to individual residents of the State of California and supplements the core Privacy Notice.

COLLECTION, USE, DISCLOSURE, SALE, AND SHARING OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

Please refer to Sections 1, 2, and 3 of the core Notice for a description of the categories of personal information collected, sources, business purposes, and categories of third parties to whom we disclose, sell, or share personal information.

DO NOT SELL OR SHARE MY PERSONAL INFORMATION

We do not sell personal information for monetary or other valuable consideration. We do share personal information with advertising partners for purposes of cross-context behavioural advertising. You have the right to opt out of the sharing of your personal information for cross-context behavioural advertising at any time by:

  • Submitting a request to privacy@spill.games; or
  • Enabling the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal on your browser or device, which we will honour as a valid opt-out for that browser or device session.

YOUR RIGHTS

Privacy Right Summary
Know and Access Request disclosure of categories and specific pieces of personal information collected sources, purposes, and categories of third parties. Up to two requests per 12-month period.
Delete Request deletion of certain personal information, subject to exceptions.
Correct Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
Opt-Out of Sale / Sharing Opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information for cross-context behavioural advertising.
Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information (SPI) We use your username and password solely to provide and secure your account and do not use or disclose them to infer characteristics about you, so the right to limit their use does not apply.
Opt-Out of Automated Decision-Making Right to opt-out of automated decision-making in certain circumstances.
Data Portability Right to receive personal information in a portable format.
Non-Discrimination Right to exercise privacy rights without discrimination.

MINORS

We do not knowingly or intentionally process the personal data of minors below the Applicable Minimum Age. Any personal data of a minor inadvertently received by us will be promptly deleted in accordance with our core Privacy Notice.

RETENTION

For each category of personal information we collect, we disclose the criteria used to determine how long it is retained: we keep each category for as long as your account is active and the category remains necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, and thereafter only as required to meet our legal, accounting, dispute-resolution and security obligations, after which it is deleted or de-identified.

HOW TO EXERCISE YOUR RIGHTS

To exercise any of your California rights, please contact us at privacy@spill.games or write to us at the physical address in the Contact Us section. We aim to respond within a reasonable timeframe as required by applicable laws of receiving your request. An authorised agent may submit requests on your behalf with written documentation.
